Hang out with us!

If you're interested in participating in events, looking for the most up-to-date information on what we're planning, or just want to chat and hang out with other queer physicists, join our slack and mailing list!

To join our mailing list: navigate to our Google groups (bConnected) page and add to your groups.

To join our slack: email officers@igenspectrum.berkeley.edu and just let us know that you're interested!

For all other questions: email officers@igenspectrum.berkeley.edu
