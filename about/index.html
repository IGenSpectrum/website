About

I•Gen•Spectrum – short for Identity and Gender Spectrum, a student group for queer phyisicists at UC Berkeley.

ei•gen•spectrum – the set of all eigenvalues of a given matrix.

IGenSpectrum was founded in 2014 by a group of Berkeley graduate students. While our membership tends to be primarily composed of graduate and undergraduate students, we are open to physicists at all levels in the department.

Our events are open to anyone who wants to participate. However, our focus and primary membership is queer and questioning physicists, and if you're not queer we ask that you consider your role in the space and to maintain the good vibes.
